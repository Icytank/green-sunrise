The **TONKER 830** is a compact and powerful pile driving machine designed for solar and construction projects. It features a **55-liter fuel tank**, **950-joule hammer power**, and stable operation on slopes up to **23% longitudinal (13°)** and **17% cross slope (10°)**. The machine has a total weight of **4,000 kg** and supports pile heights of up to **5.10 meters**.
