## The Challenge

Construction of a photovoltaic power plant with a total installed capacity of 300 kWp in Golyamo Vranovo village.

## Technical Scope

- **Installed capacity:** 300 kWp
- **Profiles:** 346 units
- **Depth:** 1 meter
- **Role:** Pile driving of supporting profiles

## The Outcome

Successfully completed pile driving of 346 steel profiles to a depth of 1 m for a photovoltaic power plant with a total installed capacity of 300 kWp.
